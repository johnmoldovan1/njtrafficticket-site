CDL Traffic Tickets

For CDL drivers, a traffic ticket can threaten more than points.

Commercial drivers and people who drive for work should not treat a New Jersey ticket as just a fine. Employer rules, disqualification risks, insurance, and record issues can be more important than the payable amount.

CDL tickets need extra caution

Some tickets that are routine for ordinary drivers can become job-threatening for CDL holders. Tailgating, speeding, reckless driving, handheld-device allegations, and accident-related charges deserve review before any plea.

Before you pay

Confirm whether the ticket is payable or requires court, check whether the charge may count as a serious traffic violation, review employer reporting obligations, and ask whether an amendment may reduce CDL risk.
