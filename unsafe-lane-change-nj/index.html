Unsafe Lane Change

Marked-lanes and unsafe lane-change tickets can carry points.

Failure to observe traffic lanes under N.J.S.A. 39:4-88 commonly carries 2 points. These tickets often appear after weaving stops, lane changes, or accidents.

What to review

Review lane markings, traffic conditions, dashcam evidence, officer observations, and whether the ticket is paired with careless driving or accident allegations.

Before paying

A 2-point lane ticket can still affect insurance or combine with prior points. Get advice if your record is not clean.
