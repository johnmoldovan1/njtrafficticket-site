Leaving the Scene

Leaving the scene is a serious traffic charge.

Leaving the scene of an accident can create significant points, insurance consequences, and court exposure. Do not handle it casually or make statements without advice.

Why this charge is different

The court may look at whether there was property damage, injury, knowledge of the accident, reporting, and prior history.

What to do now

Preserve insurance documents, photos, repair estimates, police reports, and any communications. Get advice before speaking or pleading.
