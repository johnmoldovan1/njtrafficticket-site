Insurance Impact

The insurance impact can cost more than the ticket.

A small payable fine can turn into years of higher insurance costs. Insurers may treat moving violations and points as risk signals even when the court fine looks manageable.

Get Free Advice Before Paying

Why insurance risk matters

Speeding, careless driving, reckless driving, tailgating, red-light tickets, and accident-related charges can affect underwriting or renewal decisions. Prior tickets can make the impact worse.

What to check before pleading

Check the point value, your prior record, whether an accident is involved, whether the charge can be amended, and whether a no-point result is available or sensible.

Do not focus only on the fine

The fine is a one-time amount. Insurance increases, surcharges, and license consequences can last much longer.
