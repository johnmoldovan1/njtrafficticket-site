Driving While Suspended

Driving while suspended in NJ needs immediate attention.

Driving while suspended is more serious than an ordinary moving violation. It can involve fines, additional suspension exposure, court appearances, and record consequences.

Get Free Advice Before Paying

Why this charge is serious

The court will often care why the license was suspended, whether you knew about it, and whether there are prior suspended-driving charges.

What to do before court

Find the suspension reason, check whether restoration is possible, gather MVC notices, and get advice before making statements or pleading guilty.

Quick Answers

Common questions about driving while suspended.

Can I fix the suspension before court?

Sometimes restoration steps can help, but it depends on the suspension reason and timing.

Should I go to court alone?

Get advice first because suspended-driving consequences can stack quickly.
