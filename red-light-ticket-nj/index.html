Red Light Tickets

A red light ticket can add points to your record.

Failure to observe a traffic signal commonly carries 2 MVC points when officer-issued. Accident, pedestrian, or intersection facts can make the case more serious.

What to check

Confirm the statute, whether the ticket was officer-issued, whether there was an accident, and whether a court appearance is required.

Before paying

Even a 2-point ticket can affect insurance or combine with prior points. Ask whether a lower-risk result is available.
