Cell Phone Ticket NJ

A cellphone ticket can become more serious with prior offenses.

Handheld cellphone and electronic-device tickets may look simple, but repeat offenses can create points and larger consequences.

Get Free Advice Before Paying

Why prior history matters

NJ MVC guidance treats later handheld-device offenses more seriously. If this is not your first ticket, check the point and fine consequences before paying.

What to review

Review whether the device was actually handheld, whether there are prior convictions, and whether the officer's observation can be challenged.
