Probationary Drivers

New drivers should be extra careful with traffic tickets.

A ticket that looks minor can create bigger consequences for new, young, or probationary drivers. Points and future violations can affect MVC requirements and insurance.

Get Free Advice Before Paying

Why new drivers have more risk

A first ticket can create points and a record that makes the next stop more expensive. Insurance impact can also be larger for younger drivers.

Before paying

Review whether the ticket has points, whether a no-point option exists, and how the plea could affect your license or family insurance policy.
