Careless Driving NJ

A careless driving ticket can follow you after the fine is paid.

Careless driving under N.J.S.A. 39:4-97 commonly carries 2 MVC points, but the facts matter. Accident, injury, insurance, and prior-ticket issues can make the case more serious.

What careless driving can affect

A guilty plea can add points and create a moving-violation record. If the ticket came from an accident, the practical consequences may include insurance problems, civil exposure, or additional court scrutiny.

What to check first

Review whether there was an accident or personal injury, whether there are photos or dashcam clips, whether you already have MVC points, and whether a no-point or lower-risk amendment may be available.
