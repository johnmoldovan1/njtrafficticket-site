Privacy

Privacy and intake notice.

This site collects only the information a driver chooses to submit for a free initial traffic ticket review.

Information submitted

The intake form may request a name, phone number, email address, ticket number, date of birth, driver's license number, and notes about the ticket. Do not submit information unless you want the law office to review and respond.

How information is used

Submitted information is used to evaluate the request, respond by text, email, or phone, and help determine whether legal representation should be discussed.

No attorney-client relationship by form alone

Submitting the form does not by itself create an attorney-client relationship. Representation begins only if the law office agrees to represent you and any required engagement steps are completed.

Security and retention

The site uses HTTPS in production and routes submissions to the office by configured intake tools. Avoid sending unnecessary sensitive facts. If you need immediate help, call the office directly.

NJTrafficTicket.com is an informational self-help resource and lawyer advertising.
