Improper Turn Tickets

An improper turn ticket can be a 3-point violation.

Improper right or left turn under N.J.S.A. 39:4-123 commonly carries 3 points. Intersection layout, signage, and accident facts can matter.

Get Free Advice Before Paying

Why 3 points matter

Three points can affect insurance and can become more serious if you already have points or recent tickets.

What to review

Check the exact intersection, turn lane markings, signs, officer viewpoint, and whether the charge is paired with an accident or careless driving ticket.

Common questions about improper turn tickets.

How many points is an improper turn in NJ?

Improper turn commonly carries 3 MVC points.

Should I pay an improper turn ticket?

Not before checking points, insurance, and whether an amendment is possible.
