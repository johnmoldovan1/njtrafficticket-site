Tailgating Tickets

A tailgating ticket can be a 5-point problem.

Following too closely under N.J.S.A. 39:4-89 commonly carries 5 MVC points and can be especially risky after an accident or for CDL/work drivers.

Get Free Advice Before Paying

Why tailgating is high risk

Five points can raise insurance and license concerns. If the ticket came after a rear-end accident, practical consequences may go beyond the traffic fine.

Before you pay

Review distance, speed, traffic conditions, accident facts, dashcam evidence, and whether a lower-point resolution may be available.
